FAQs
  • ❓ How to cast vote?
    Go to the 'Cast Vote' page, select an election and candidate, then submit.
  • ❓ Can I change my vote?
    No, once submitted, a vote cannot be changed.
  • ❓ How to see voting history?
    Go to the 'Voting History' page under your user dashboard.
